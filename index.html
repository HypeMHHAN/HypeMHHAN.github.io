Carl is Your HYPEMHHAN & Energy Personifed

An All-American, Conference Champion, Successful Business Owner, Proven University Leader, and a Family-Oriented Achievement Enthusiast, Carl Thomas embodies a powerful boost of energy and offers his audiences an electrifying delivery of motivational principles and lessons.

Lively Presentations That Inspire Beyond Momentary Excitement

Your Motivational Hip Hop Animated Narrator provides decades of life lessons, shares success clues and illustrates proven tactics to help people of varying abilities and back grounds to identify WHAT they want, WHY they want it and HOW to achieve any goal. Carl, also, shares how previous goals, often, manifest into new aspirations that develop throughout the process of pursuing ones’ best life.

Jump

Sometimes you have to fly before you can walk… There is no perfect time to get started on your dreams. You just have to go for it and trust that when adjustments are necessary you will adapt to what is needed to keep moving forward. Make a decision, be dedicated, stay persistent, learn from failure - never give up! JUMP! Right Now! If you want, I’ll be there with you for the jump.

Work

Opportunity often shows up disguised as hard work! The truth is that opportunities are those places and times in our lives where hard work meets a chance to progress. So, keep working hard so that you are prepared when your chance presents itself to you. You’d much rather be prepared and waiting for an opportunity than to be presented with an opportunity and NOT be prepared for it.

Live

Live your best life! Don’t disregard the process or the pursuit of your dreams. Reaching your goals (aka Victory) is, ABSOLUTELY, sweet! However, the journey is what you get to savor far longer than the time you get to experience winning. Appreciate where you are along the path to your greatness; even the pain will be remembered with reverence when you, finally, conquer your dragon.

Share

There are not ANY selfless “good deeds.” So, give what time and resources you have to help other champions “push their rocks” and “climb their mountains.” By giving to other people and helping them work on their goals, you will tap into an unlimited source of self-sustaining power that all people have access to and can draw upon to recharge their own Discipline and Will to be great.
